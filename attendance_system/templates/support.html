Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I enroll in a course? ▼

To enroll in a course, navigate to the 'Available Courses' section, select the course you want to enroll in, and click the 'Enroll' button.

2. How do I use facial recognition to take attendance? ▼

The Face recogntion model takes the individual student face encodings when creating the student account. When a student captures an image, if the encoding matches the encoding of a student enrolled under the course, the student is marked present
