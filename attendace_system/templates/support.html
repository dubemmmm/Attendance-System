Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I enroll in a course? ▼

To enroll in a course, navigate to the 'Available Courses' section, select the course you want to enroll in, and click the 'Enroll' button.

2. How do I use facial recognition to log in? ▼

Click on the 'Login with Facial Recognition' button on the login page, and follow the on-screen instructions to log in using facial recognition.
